Chicken Leg Quarters

Image source: Royalty-free stock photo

Description

This is the old cooking method I used early in grad school when I was obsessed with trying random cooking methods off the Internet. When this one worked, I could stop frantically searching.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Leg Quarters, as many as you are going to eat (Probably 2 or 3)

Tools

  • 1 baking pan for roasting the chicken
  • 1 baking pan of equal or larger size to cover the roasting pan
  • Potholder

Steps

  1. Bring the chicken to room temperature for a couple of hours. Rinse the chicken and place it in your roasting pan. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  2. Place the roasting pan, covered, into the hot oven for 60 minutes.
  3. After 60 minutes, remove the cover and cook for another 30 minutes, still at 350° F.
  4. After the total of 90 minutes, your food is ready. I let it stand 10 minutes before eating, but who knows if that was necessary.
